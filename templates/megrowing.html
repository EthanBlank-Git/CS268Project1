2 Days

The hoglets were just born and currently unable to see. Recommended not to disturb the mother hedgehog at all so she doesnt abondon the hoglets.

5 Days

The hoglets are still unable to see however you can do some careful spot cleaning of their cage.

8 Days

At this point the hoglets are more likely to survive but be careful to not disturb the mother in any way.

11 Days

You should be able to clean the cages as long as the mom doesnt seem to be in distressed.

15 Days

At this point up until the 18th day is when the hoglets gain the ability to see.

21 Days

Almost through the hardpart, it isn't very likely for the mother to abandon them at this point and they hoglets will start moving around more.

24 Days

The hoglets have grown a fair amount and in the following week you can begin with weaning process.
