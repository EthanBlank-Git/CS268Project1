Q: Can hedgehogs live together

A: I do not cage my hedgehogs together but they will (sometimes) accept companions I only recommend housing females together because males will fight one another.


Q: Do they sleep during the day?

A: Yes they are nocturnal and will sleep during the day and are very active at night. They may get up from time to time during the day to get something to eat and drink especially the ones with babies.


Q: Are hedgehogs expensive?

A: To buy one can get pricey, to care for it not really unless it has to go to the vet.


Q: Do hedgehogs need a wheel in their cage?

A: Yes. Hedgehogs can get really restless.
